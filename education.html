Education

Jake Belnap

I have a solid educational background, combining both technical and scientific expertise. I earned an Associate of Applied Science and an Associate of Science, both with Summa Cum Laude honors, demonstrating my commitment to academic excellence. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering at Brigham Young University and am an active member of AIChE, reflecting my engagement in the field.

My undergraduate research includes work on lithium-ion battery technology, where I synthesized and tested electrospun lithium-titanate ultrafine fibers to improve electrochemical performance. Additionally, I contributed to developing a chemical-powered car using hydrogen fuel cells and iodine clock reactions for a regional AIChE conference. These experiences highlight my strong technical foundation and proficiency in areas such as electrochemistry, chemical reactions, and engineering design.
